Through Ariadne’s Thread * A Hundred Poems

In the Mind of Artur Nowak

Chapter 1

*@* The wheels of a fiery chariot

beckon me into the space of nothingness.

Jupiter’s rings streak around me.

I long for this woman,

but she is already weaving braids of starlight across the galaxy.

Ariadne’s thread in the labyrinth—

break the silence
